How It Works

Three steps to every safe journey

01

Enter Auto Number

Before boarding, type the auto registration number. Get the driver's full details, safety rating, license status, and any complaints - instantly.

02

Share Live Journey

Your 5 trusted contacts get your real-time location, route map, auto number, and driver details - automatically the moment you start a journey.

03

Stay Protected Always

Smart alerts for danger zones, one-tap SOS, 30-second safety checks, and automatic alerts if your phone switches off mid-journey.

Features

Everything you need on every ride

Instant Vehicle Verification

Enter any auto number and get full driver details, registration status, past complaints, and a community safety rating in seconds.

Safe Route Intelligence

Before you travel, see which routes are safe and which are danger zones - covering all cities and worldwide maps.

Danger Zone Alerts

Instant notification if your auto enters a flagged area. 30-second countdown - no response means automatic SOS to all contacts.

5 Emergency Contacts

Set up to 5 trusted people. They get your auto details, live location, and instant SOS alerts - all with one tap from you.

Phone Off = Auto Alert

If your phone switches off during a journey, all emergency contacts are immediately notified with your last known location.
